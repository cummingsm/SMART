Upload a list of barcodes for processing in a text file.

Step 1

  • The text file should have one barcode per line.
  • Spaces or letters should not be present in the file.
  • Do not have any blank lines at the end of the file.
  • Do not include more than 100 lines of barcodes.
  • After you click the Submit button, your file will be uploaded.
  • If the upload was successful, you will be prompted to continue processing the file.
